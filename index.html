Vision

empowered diversity

People understand and respect different cultures and societies. The environment of empowered diversity supports people in applying their full potential and acting responsibly.

Mission

developing students

BEST helps students to achieve an international mindset, to reach a better understanding of cultures and societies and to develop the capacity to work in culturally diverse environments.

Case Study

The teams have the opportunity to analyse and solve a problem based on a real situation. To do that the participants have access to the research tools such as internet and they must present their solution after 6 to 7 hours of working. The participant profile should include following things: – Critical Thinking – Solution Techniques – Creativity – Be logistically compatible

Team Design

Team design is a practical challenge that requires the design, creation and presentation of a prototype model that can successfully meet specific construction and operation criteria, all done in a limited amount of tima and through the use of limited resources (metarials and/or software). -Be practical -Be creative -Be economic -Be applicable

TERMS OF APPLICATION

● You have to participate with your 3 friends as a group.Totally 4+1 people (+1 replacement, optional) ● All of group members should be Yıldız Technical University students. ● You have to keep your camera and microphone on during the competition. ● Everyone has to speak English during the competition and presentation.

FAQ

● Does the competitors’ study field matter?

No, it does not.

● Do I have to pay a fee for the contest?

There are no fees inclueded in any round.

● How will the scoring be done?

Scoring will be done by our school’s professors.

● Can I apply for both team design and case study?

Yes, you can.

● Is there any training sessions besides competition, do I have to attend these sessions?

There will be company presentations which are mandatory for the competitors to attend.

● What can I earn from this competition?

If you win the local round you will have a chance to compete in the national round. After winning the National Round, you can compete in the EBEC Final and if you win that, you can call yourself the best engineer in the Europe. In addition, you will get participant certificate and if you end up in the top 3, you will get a certificate of achievement.

Turk Telekom

180 yıllık köklü bir geçmişe sahip olan Türk Telekom, Türkiye’nin ilk entegre telekomünikasyon operatörüdür. Müşterilerin hızla değişen iletişim ve teknoloji ihtiyaçlarına en güçlü ve en doğru şekilde cevap verebilmek amacıyla 2015 yılında Türk Telekomünikasyon A.Ş., TT Mobil İletişim Hizmetleri A.Ş. ve TTNET A.Ş. tüzel kişiliklerini mevcut şekliyle muhafaza ederek ve tabi oldukları mevzuat ve regülasyonlara tamamen uyarak, “müşteri odaklı” ve entegre bir yapıya geçmiştir. Bireysel ve kurumsal hizmetler alanında geniş hizmet ağı ve zengin ürün çeşitliliğine sahip olan Türk Telekom, Ocak 2016 itibarıyla mobil, internet, telefon ve TV ürün ve hizmetlerini ‘Türk Telekom’ tek marka çatısı altında bir araya getirmiştir.
