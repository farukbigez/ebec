BEST, Board of European Students of Technology is a constantly growing non-profit and non political organization. Since 1989 we provide communucation, cooperation and exchange possibilities for students all over Europe.

student reached

Vision

empowered diversity

People understand and respect different cultures and societies. The environment of empowered diversity supports people in applying their full potential and acting responsibly.

Mission

developing students

BEST helps students to achieve an international mindset, to reach a better understanding of cultures and societies and to develop the capacity to work in culturally diverse environments.

Values

BEST Spirit

BEST Spirit is the inspiration for all our actions. The 5 values carrying us forward are;FLEXIBILITY, FRIENDSHIP, LEARNING IMPROVMENT and FUN.

Founded in 2008, LBG (Local BEST Group) Istanbul Yıldız has been an active and hardworking member of BEST ever since. We’re an enthusiastic and dynamic group of friends mainly composed of engineering students at Yildiz Technical University. With over 80 active members we are one of the strongest teams in Europe. Yearly, we organise 12 local and international events which are very popular among students from all universities in Istanbul. Our events are largely attended by over 6 000 students every year. For more information please visit our website.

Engineering competitions are the events we organise where student groups use their technical, social and communication skills to perform a specific task. In these competitions, under certain conditions (time, situation, materials, etc.), students compete to provide the most appropriate solution in terms of performance, cost and innovation. There are two different categories for EBEC: -CASE STUDY -TEAM DESIGN
